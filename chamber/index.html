How To Join Us

  1. Show proof of being Genuine Business person or group operating within our town and environs.
  2. Obtain the chambers application form from the secretariat.
  3. Be sponsored by two numbers of the council of the chamber (the council list is available on request).
  4. Complete the application form from our form portal or come to our office, complete the form and return it with the first year’s subscription, levies & charges along with your company’s certification and form.
  5. Attend a briefing meeting on invitation.
