Purpose

My area of goal is to unify local traders, assisit businesses with marketing, attract loans and grant for them, help in promoting small businesses of every sector by organizing seminars and symposium, help to attract foreign investors, export local made products and finally regulate the prices of goods and services.

Audience

My targeted audience are the local businesses in my town like the Tailors, the traders, the warehouse sectors, the engineering sectors, the carpentery and upholstry. However, if you are from the age of 14 and above, have any high school certificate, have a smart phone or desktop, you are eligible to benefit from our organization.
